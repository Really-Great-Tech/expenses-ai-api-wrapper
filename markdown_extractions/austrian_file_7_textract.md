The Passenger Receipts issued by Austrian Airlines are compliant with requirements stipulated by Austrian VAT law (Sec 11 (9) VAT act "Fahrausweise")
and thus constitute valid invoices in accordance with Sec 11 VAT act. The Passenger Receipt can be used to claim travel expenses and input VAT in
combination with the corresponding credit card slip or debit slip. It is not necessary to state the full name or address of the recipient on tickets
("Fahrausweise") in accordance to Sec 11 (9) VAT act. The name stated on the ticket indicates the passenger entitled to travel and not the recipient. Please
note that VAT is only applicable on domestic flights. Tickets and passenger receipts issued for travel on international flights do not show any VAT, as the
services are VAT exempt.
